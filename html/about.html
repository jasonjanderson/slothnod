Why another twitter star aggregation site?

Slothnod is an alternative to the predominant star aggregation sites like Favstar and pre-edited comedy sites such as Witstream. It's not for everyone, nor does it aim to be. SlothNod especially hopes to provide a forum for newer, less-followed people, people who use Twitter in any number of less star-popular ways, people who aren't consciously writing comedy, and people with less ambition than it takes to compete for leaderboard status elsewhere. Ideally, SlothNod will be a place to discover new intriguing Twitites, a place where there might be a broader variety of writing along with the people's favorite comedy, and a place where members of am ersatz community can chill out without so much competition, cliques, castes, clamoring for position, and other all-too-human behaviors evidenced on Favstar currently and on Favrd before that. Or so the sloth hopes. Once the site is up, it will become whatever people make of it.

Why SLOTHNOD? That's an awful name.

The sloth is a slow-moving, unambitious creature. A nod is a slight move of the head indicating approval, understanding, or agreement. Having observed the rather feverish starring behavior and jockeying for position on the predominant Twitter star sites for the last two years, a more relaxed, content-driven Twitter star collector seems like a decent idea. It might be a shitty idea. The sloth will see. it depends on the people who join in.

What makes Slothnod different?

  • SLOTHNOD IS AN OPT-IN FORUM. People manually sign up to join SlothNod. Nods are only collected from, and for, SlothNodders.
  • CONTENT FIRST - The authors of a blurt (tweet) will not be idetnified on the day their blurt makes the SlothNod scroll. Identities will be revealed each dat at 12:00 a.m. GMT when that day's scroll (a.k.a. board) is closed. Anonymity might be fun. Or not.
  • LEVEL PLAYING FIELD - SlothNod will attempt to level the social hierarchy that has slowly developed since Twitter launched, was cemented, and built upon, by the launch of Favrd (May 2008), and continued now by Favstar, the predominant Twitter site.
  • SIMPLE, FAIR FORMULA - A Slothnodder's blurt will appear on the scroll when s/he receives nods (stars) from 1/2 of 1 percent of their total followers plus "followings." Simple math: # of followers + # followed X .005 = SlothNod Scroll appearance.

The sloth also expects not to care. The sloth wants to run this site on the cheap. For fun. And discovery.
  • NUMBER OF FOLLOWERS + FOLLOWINGS TO JOIN SLOTHNOD = LESS THAN 50,000. (We might lower this number before launch, to maybe 10,000.) SlothNod isn't for celebrities.

  • FILTERED OFF SLOTHNOD SCROLL: @replies, tweets with @username (mentions), #HASHTAGS, tweets with links. Twitter is wonderful for social interaction, sharing, marketing and circulating one's crippling crush on Justine Beaver ad nauseam. The Slothnod scroll is for original, isolated blurts mailed to the ether, like an unaddressed postcard. Words. Your words. That's what matters to the sloth.
