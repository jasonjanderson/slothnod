Welcome to Slothnod, a free, opt-in Twitter favorite aggregation site and ersatz community.

SlothNod is different:

  • MEMBERSHIP - For individuals with than 50,000 total followers plus those you're following. Nods are only collected from (and for) members.
  • ANONYMITY - Authors of a blurt (tweet) are not identified until the following day.
  • NO BANNED LANGUAGE - SlothNod is not for children or prudes.
  • GRADUATED FORMULA - (Following + Followers) x .005 = Nods (from members) for your blurt to appear on board.
  • SLOTHNOD IS FOR FUN, NOT PROFIT in either cold or social currency.
