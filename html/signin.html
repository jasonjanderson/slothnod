Welcome. Slothnod is a Twitter content aggregator of blurts selected by members of its virtual, proto-quasi-town in the innerwebz ether. Slothnod is different from previous Twitter favorite aggegators or "star" sites:

  • Membership is voluntary (opt-in) and continued (opt-out) by your choice.
  • All blurts on SlothNod are anonymous for 1 day.
  • Appearance of a blurt on the Nod Board is based on this simple math:
    A Slothnodder's blurt will appear on the Nod Board when s/he receives (# of followers * ) or nods (whichever is greater) on a blurt from from fellow Slothnodders.
  • All blurts containing @replies, or links are filtered out.
  • SlothNod is NC-17 and up. It's not for children or prudes. No words are banned.

SlothNod language

  • Blurt = A tweet.
  • Nod = A star or favorite on a tweet.
  • Nodder / SlothNodder = A member of SlothNod.

Fine Print

  • Slothnod is not responsible for the content of an individual's blurt.
  • The only data we fetch from Twitter is your favorites and follower count.
  • We will never tweet or follow on your behalf.
  • We cannot see your Twitter password.
  • We will never read your DMs.
  • You may leave SlothNod and revoke access to your account at any time via Twitter's connections page.
